General Information about Overall Strategies which Treat Multiple Problem Areas

Glossary | Menu Shortcut Page

Over the years people working in the conflict resolution and peace making fields have developed a large number of comprehensive strategies for dealing with difficult conflict problems.  These approaches typically combine several of the treatment strategies  described earlier in his training program.  In addition, many strategies cannot be categorized simply as force-based, integrative, or exchange-based strategies, because they are a combination of two or three of these approaches.  The following section contains descriptions of these combination strategies as well as information about how each approach relates to the ideas presented in this training program.
